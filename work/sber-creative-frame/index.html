SBER Creative Frame

Combine

In 2020, Sber completely changed its positioning, removing the "bank" label and transforming into a full-fledged ecosystem of services. The new brand united technology, convenience, and user care — embedding these values into its design. After a few years, the design system required further fine-tuning. This led to the development of a new creative framework — a visual language focused on 3D, designed to strengthen relationships with users and refresh Sber's visual communication. Together with Combine studio, I have developed many unique images that helped Sber to change its visual style.
